We are all about being Natural!

We sell 100% plastic-free, natural and biodegradable products.

ECO-FRIENDLY:

Our rolls are made from 100% organic bamboo, which is a fast growing grass giving a sustainable, natural alternative to traditional tree based toilet paper rolls. Unlike trees which can take up to 30 years to grow, bamboo grows back in as quickly as 3 to 4 months.

CHEMICAL-FREE:

Our bamboo toilet rolls are Paraben-free, BPA free, chlorine free and hypoallergenic causing no skin rashes. They are made of pure bamboo and are highly absorbent.

SUITABLE FOR ALL DISPOSAL SYSTEMS:

The toilet rolls are suitable and all drainage systems as they are highly absorbent and biodegradable in nature. Clog free for disposal systems.

BIODEGRADABLE:

ECOSOFT toilet rolls are environmental-friendly and compostable in nature. They biodegrade in the soil within 2-6 months and have no toxic elements.

PACKAGING:

Plastic packaging is one of the top contributors to plastic pollution. All of our products have eco-friendly packaging and are delivered plastic-free. Primary, secondary and transport packaging are sustainable and use the least amount of plastic we can.
